Admission Eligibility

Educational Qualification

Minimum SSC or equivalent passing certificate from any recognized board.

Age Limit

Applicant must be between 16 to 30 years of age at the time of application.

Health & Fitness

Must possess sound physical and mental health for technical workshops.

Need Help?

If you have any questions regarding the admission process, our helpline is open from 9 AM to 5 PM.
